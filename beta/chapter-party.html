MISO DRESSING

MISO DRESSING
  • 4 tsp miso
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ pickling onion, peeled and chopped
  • 25ml rice wine vinegar
  • 50ml canola oil
  • 3 tsp sugar
  • 7ml sesame oil
  • 10ml sake
  • 10ml mirin
BEEF
  • 240g hanger steak or any other lean tender beef cut
  • 4 button mushroom, stalks left on and thinly sliced
  • 4 baby turnip, thinly sliced
  • 5g cocoa nibs, blitzed or crushed in mortar & pestle
  • micro herbs & flowers
Miso Dressing

Combine all ingredients for the dressing and blitz with a stick blender until smooth.
