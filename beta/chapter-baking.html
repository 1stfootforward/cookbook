Daffodil Cake

  • 4 egg whites (room temp)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ cup caster sugar
  • ¼ tsp vanilla
  • ½ - ¾ cup self-raising flour
  • ¼ cup caster sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp grated orange rind
  • 1 tbsp orange juice
  • 2 drops almond essence

Prepare a flat bottom round tin by buttering and flouring the bottom and sides. Pre-heat the oven 190°C.

Beat the egg whites with salt until frothy. Add the first measure of caster sugar and vanilla, and continue to beat until thick. Stop before the peaks form when the beater is removed (do not over mix).
