Tomato Chicken with Zoodles

CHICKEN
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 6 anchovy fillets (optional)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 250ml chicken stock
  • 100g kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1 whole chicken, butterflied
GREMOLATA
  • handful Italian parsley, chopped
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 1 - 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • extra virgin olive oil
TO SERVE
  • 1 courgette per serve
Chicken

Preheat the oven 180°C.

Heat olive oil in a large frypan over a medium heat. Sauté the onion and garlic until transparent, add the brown sugar, and caramelise to a light brown colour. Add the anchovies, tomato paste, chopped tomatoes, chicken stock and olives, simmer for 5 minutes.
