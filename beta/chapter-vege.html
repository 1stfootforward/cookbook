Roast Kumara Platter with Bean Tagine

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large Beauregard (Orange) kumara
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ brown onion, peeled and diced
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • ¼ tsp chilli paste, or to taste
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp fresh parsley
  • ¼ tsp fresh oregano
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  • 1 400g tin mixed beans (4 bean mix), drained and rinsed
  • 1 400g tin chopped tomatoes,
  • 1 tsp vegetable stock powder
  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 2 tbsp buckwheat
  • 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds, toasted
  • ½ lemon, zested
  • salt and pepper to taste

Mint leaves, thinly sliced.

Pre-heat the oven to 200°C. Peel and slice the kumara into ¾ cm thick slices. Rub with 2 tbsp olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Roast in a roasting dish for approximately 20 minutes until just tender. Remove from the oven and keep warm….
