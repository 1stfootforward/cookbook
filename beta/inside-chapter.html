Homemade Baked beans

  • 1 tin white beans (cannellini, haricot or any other beans of your choice), drained & rinsed
  • 1 medium sized onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tin crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tsp chilli sauce
  • 4 eggs

Preheat the oven 150°C.
