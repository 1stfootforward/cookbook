Salmon Mousse

MOUSSE
  • 4 gelatine leaves
  • 300g fresh salmon fillet, deboned and skin removed
  • 4 shallots, diced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 300ml fresh cream
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • white pepper to taste
  • few drops Tabasco sauce
TO SERVE
  • 100g smoked salmon
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 2 tbsp chives, finely chopped plus extra to garnish
  • Ciabatta or rustic bread, sliced & toasted
Mousse

Soften the gelatine leaves in a small bowl of cold water.

Poach the salmon in a covered saucepan with water over a low heat for 5-6 minutes until cooked, remove from the water and cool. Retain ½ cup of the fish poaching liquid….
