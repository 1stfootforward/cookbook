Raspberry Meringue Tarts

Macaroon Pastry
  • 200g desiccated coconut
  • 150g caster sugar
  • 70g plain flour
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
Raspberry Curd
  • 250g raspberries, fresh or frozen
  • 3 tsp cold water
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 90g unsalted butter, chopped
  • 150g caster sugar
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • 2 whole eggs, lightly beaten
  • 5 egg yolks, lightly beaten
Meringue
  • 4 egg whites
  • ¾ cup caster sugar
Macaroon Pastry

In a large mixing bowl combine the coconut, sugar and flour. Add the beaten eggs and mix well.

Wrap the pastry in plastic wrap and rest in the fridge overnight. Next day roll out the pastry between two sheets of baking paper, and press into a 23cm round loose bottom tart tin or small individual tins, trimming the edges. Rest in the fridge for 1 hour….
